About Shypram Enterprise

Shypram Enterprise, based in Bhosari, Pune (Maharashtra, India), is a trusted name in the supply, distribution, and import-export of high-quality electrical and industrial automation components. With a strong focus on reliability and performance, we cater to a wide range of industrial requirements, particularly in switchgear systems and electrical equipment.

Operating from our facility at Morya Industrial Estate, MIDC Bhosari, we are committed to delivering quality products that meet industry standards and customer expectations. Under the leadership of Mr. Bhavesh Kucha (Business Head), Shypram Enterprise continues to build strong relationships with clients through dependable service and competitive pricing.

We offer a comprehensive range of products including Proximity Switches, Photoelectric Sensors, Rotary Encoders, Fiber Optic Sensors and Cables, Ultrasonic Sensors, Capacitive Sensors, Magnetic Switches, Pressure Sensors, Level Switches, Flow Switches, Temperature Controllers, Counters, Timers, Relays, SSRs, Relay Cards, Power Supplies, SMPS, Limit Switches, Micro Switches, Plugs & Sockets, Induction Motors, Stepper Motor Drives, and more.

Our goal is to provide complete electrical and automation solutions that enhance operational efficiency and ensure long-term reliability for our customers.
